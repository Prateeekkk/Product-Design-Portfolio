Spec Whisperer Beta

Drop in a Slack thread, a meeting transcript, or three voice notes. Get back a 1-pager: problem, scope, constraints, open questions. Designers can stop being amateur stenographers.

Naming Things Live · for fun

12 names. 11 are bad. By design.

Most "AI naming tools" generate options that all sound the same. This one deliberately ranges from terrible to interesting, so the "good" one is obvious by contrast. Naming is a contrast game.

Brief: An invoice tool for freelancers
Picks: Receiptly, Bill.io, Tally, InvoiceBro, FundFlow…
